Our Journey

Real-time Monitoring

Real-time monitoring continuously analyzes the driver's facial features and eye movements using AI and computer vision. This instant detection of drowsiness allows for immediate alerts, ensuring the driver remains awake and alert, thereby significantly enhancing safety and preventing fatigue-related accidents.

Alert System

The alert system activates when drowsiness is detected, using sounds, seat vibrations, and visual signals to wake the driver. This multi-sensory approach ensures drivers stay attentive, effectively preventing fatigue-related accidents and enhancing overall road safety.

Facial Recognition

Facial recognition and eye detection technologies monitor the driver's face and eye movements in real time. These features identify signs of drowsiness, such as drooping eyelids or prolonged blinking, enabling the system to issue timely alerts and enhance driver safety.

Integration with Vehicle Systems

Integration with vehicle systems enables seamless communication between ZDRiver and onboard sensors. This allows for real-time data exchange, enhancing the accuracy of drowsiness detection and enabling proactive safety measures, such as adjusting seat positions or activating hazard lights, to mitigate potential risks.

Driver Profiling

Driver profiling customizes monitoring and alert settings based on individual behaviors, ensuring personalized safety features tailored to each driver's needs.

Eye Tracking

Monitoring the driver's eye movements to detect signs of drowsiness, such as drooping eyelids or prolonged blinking.

Data Logging

records driving behavior and alert triggers, enabling analysis and review to enhance the system's effectiveness and safety measures.
